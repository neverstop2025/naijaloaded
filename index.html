[Music] Sizzy Benjamin – I Go Win
by Naijaloaded. on 25 March, 2025 under Music

Fast-rising Afrobeat sensation Sizzy Benjamin is back with a powerful new track “I Go Win” under Red Nation Records.

This inspiring anthem is set to resonate with listeners, delivering a message of perseverance, determination, and self-confidence.
With a blend of vibrant Afrobeat rhythms and soul-stirring lyrics, “I Go Win” encourages anyone facing obstacles to keep pushing forward. Sizzy Benjamin‘s unique sound, infectious energy, and heartfelt delivery make this a must-listen for fans of motivational music.
